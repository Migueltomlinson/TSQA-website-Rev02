Our Services

From first risk assessment to full quality management everything your business needs to operate safely, compliantly, and confidently.

🛡️
Risk & Safety Management
Risk Assessments

Every workplace carries risk. We identify, evaluate, and document the hazards specific to your operations, giving you a clear picture of where your business is exposed and what needs to be done about it. Our risk assessments are practical, site-specific, and built to meet your legal obligations under the Health and Safety at Work Act 2015.

Workplace Safety Inspections

A fresh set of expert eyes can catch what familiarity misses. We conduct thorough workplace inspections, identifying hazards, unsafe practices, and compliance gaps before they become incidents. You receive a clear, actionable report so issues get resolved, not overlooked.

Site Safety Plans & Risk Assessments

Before work begins, the right plan protects everyone on site. We develop comprehensive site safety plans tailored to your project scope, ensuring your team, contractors, and principal know exactly how hazards will be managed from day one.

⚙️
Safety Systems & Preparedness
Safety Management Systems Development

A well-built safety management system is the foundation of a safe and compliant business. We design and implement systems that are practical for your team to use, fit for your industry, and structured to meet both regulatory requirements and pre-qualification standards.

Emergency Preparedness & Response Plans

When an emergency happens, your team needs to know exactly what to do. We develop clear, site-specific emergency response plans that cover likely scenarios, assign responsibilities, and ensure your people can respond quickly and confidently when it matters most.

Emergency Drills

A plan is only as good as the people who can execute it. We design and facilitate emergency drills that test your response procedures in a controlled environment, identify weaknesses, and build confidence across your team.

🔬
Incident & Culture Improvement
Incident & Accident Investigation

When something goes wrong, understanding why is critical to making sure it does not happen again. We conduct thorough, impartial investigations that go beyond surface causes, identifying root causes and delivering recommendations that drive real improvement.

Safety Culture Assessment

Culture determines how safety really works in your business, not policies, not posters. We assess how safety is lived and led across your organisation, giving you an honest picture of where your culture stands and what is holding it back.

Safety Culture Improvement

We work alongside your leadership and teams to shift safety from a compliance exercise to a genuine value. Through practical engagement, targeted interventions, and measurable goals, we help build a culture where people look out for each other and safety becomes part of how work gets done.

📁
Contractor & Pre-Qualification Support
Contractor & Supplier Safety Management

The contractors you engage are your responsibility. We help you build robust systems for selecting, onboarding, and monitoring contractors and suppliers, ensuring the people working alongside your team meet your health and safety standards at every stage.

IMPAC PREQUAL

IMPAC PREQUAL is used by councils, government agencies, and large organisations to verify that a contractor has the right health and safety systems in place before work is awarded. It focuses on how your systems perform in practice, not just what is written in your documents. TSQA assesses your existing documentation, identifies gaps, builds your evidence base, and prepares your submission for a successful PREQUAL outcome.

SiteWise

SiteWise is administered by Site Safe New Zealand and grades a contractor's health and safety capability. That grade is visible to main contractors and principal organisations, directly influencing whether you get selected for work. TSQA works with you to achieve a strong SiteWise grade, giving you a measurable advantage when tendering.

SHE Pre-Qual

SHE Pre-Qual is widely used across the building and construction sector, with many councils requiring contractors to hold a valid SHE assessment before undertaking high-risk or long-term work. TSQA helps you build and evidence an active safety management system that meets SHE Pre-Qual standards, not just on paper, but demonstrably in practice.

Tōtika

Tōtika is New Zealand's national health and safety pre-qualification scheme, developed by CHASNZ to create one consistent standard across the construction and contracting sector. Complete it once and have it recognised across multiple clients and industries. TSQA guides you through the Tōtika assessment from start to finish, helping you achieve certification that is recognised nationally.

📊
Auditing & Compliance
Internal Audits

Regular internal audits keep your health and safety systems honest. We conduct structured audits of your processes, procedures, and records, measuring performance against your own standards and identifying where improvements are needed before an external party does.

Supplier & External Audits

Your supply chain is an extension of your business. We audit your suppliers and external parties against your health and safety requirements, giving you confidence that the organisations you work with are meeting the standards you expect.

Hazardous Substances Registers

Managing hazardous substances is a legal requirement and a genuine safety obligation. We develop and maintain accurate registers of all hazardous substances in your workplace, ensuring you have full visibility of what is on site, where it is, and how it must be handled.

SDS Management

Safety Data Sheets must be current, accessible, and understood by the people who need them. We manage your SDS library, keeping it up to date and organised so your team always has the right information at hand.

🏅
Quality Assurance
ISO 9001:2015 QMS Implementation

ISO 9001:2015 is the international standard for quality management systems. We guide you through implementation from the ground up, establishing processes, documentation, and controls that improve consistency, reduce errors, and demonstrate quality to your clients.

Internal Auditing

We conduct structured internal audits of your quality management system, assessing conformance, identifying nonconformances, and providing clear recommendations to keep your QMS performing and your certification secure.

Inspection & Test Plan (ITP) Development

An ITP defines exactly what gets inspected, when, by whom, and to what standard. We develop detailed, project-specific ITPs that provide a clear quality framework for your team and give clients confidence that work is being checked at every critical stage.

Manufacturing Data Report (MDR) Compilation

MDRs bring together all the quality documentation for a project or product into one complete record. We compile accurate, well-organised MDRs that satisfy client and contract requirements and provide a reliable audit trail from start to finish.

Visual Weld Inspection

Weld quality is critical in any fabrication or construction environment. Our experienced inspectors carry out visual weld inspections to identify surface defects, poor workmanship, and non-conformances, ensuring welds meet the applicable standards and project specifications.

Welding Procedure Specification (WPS) Verification

A WPS defines how welding must be carried out to achieve the required result. We verify that your welding procedure specifications are fit for purpose, correctly documented, and aligned with the applicable codes and standards.

Welder Qualifications

Using qualified welders is not just best practice, it is often a contractual and regulatory requirement. We assess and verify welder qualifications to confirm your team has the competency to carry out the work to the required standard.

Material Traceability & Certification Review

Knowing where your materials came from and that they meet specification is fundamental to quality control. We review material certifications and traceability documentation to ensure what has been supplied matches what was specified and ordered.

Inspection & Test Record (ITR) Management

ITRs are the evidence that inspections and tests have been carried out correctly. We manage your ITR process from creation through to completion, ensuring records are accurate, complete, and ready to support project handover and client sign-off.

Document Creation

We create clear, professional, and fit-for-purpose documents tailored to your business and industry. Whether you need policies, procedures, forms, registers, or plans, we build documentation that your team can actually use, written in plain language, structured logically, and aligned to the relevant standards and legislation.

Document Review

Existing documents can become outdated, inconsistent, or no longer fit for purpose as your business evolves. We review your current documentation against applicable standards, legal requirements, and best practice — identifying gaps, inconsistencies, and areas for improvement, then updating them to reflect how your business actually operates.
